Shree KalyanraijiSarvajanik Charitable Trust



An Academy for research and development on manuscript preservation and interpretations. Inspire and assimilate creations of Cultural heritage in form of Devotional Art. Literature and Music by organizing camps and competitions. Under this trust, KALYANPRASAAD has been constructed in the heart of city which comprises the facilities like Pushti Prabha Medical and Health Center, A beautiful Dhyan Mandir, Huge and Sophisticated Vallabh Satsang Hall, Art Exhibition Gallery, Yadunath Computer Education and Research Center, Vallabhaashray - (Vishram Gruh) and Acharya Nivas etc. In KALYANPRASAAD, as a part of Medical Activity - Physiotherapy Unit, Acupressure, Homeopathy and Dental Unit has been working. As a Educational Unit of Computer Center since last year Yadunath Computer Center is working under which it provides Computer education of Basic, Tally and many other courses.
