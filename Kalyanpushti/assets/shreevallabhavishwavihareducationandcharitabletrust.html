Venunaad

Venunaad is a divine expression of Love and a creation of the Lord Shree Krishna.

Goal and Vision:

In modern trend of Western and POP music has almost eclipsed the traditional ancient music of India. To protect and rehabilitate Indian classical music it must be promoted at all levels of life. To bring awareness among the youth of the nation they must be motivated towards to motivate for our Indian music and its rich heritage. The divinity of Venunaad must thus be preserved through repeated performances by experts in classical music and flushed in suitably into modern film music if possible to popularize it.
