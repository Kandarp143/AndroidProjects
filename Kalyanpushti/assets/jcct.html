Jayantilal Chand Charitable Trust



Pushtidham (A Pilgrimage of Hindu Vedic Sanatan Dharma according to Pushtimargiya Tradition) India is a holy land for the various incarnations of the Almighty. From the time immemorial India has always been an enormous source for igniting that flame of spirituality across the globe; especially the people of Gujarat have contributed massively in nurturing Indian spiritual tradition in each and every individual. Gujaratis are better known for inspiring devotional hearts for the activities of the welfare of the human beings and their society through the medium of spiritualism. Godhara (Panch Mahal-Gujarat) is well known for Karma, Gyan and Bhakti where practitioners of Pushti-Bhakti traditions like Shri Mahaprabhuji, Shri Gunsaiji, and Shri Ghanshyamlalji had enlightened all by sawing the seeds of Pushtimarg and sharing the fruits of the same respectively. Pushtidham, based on Pushti-Bhakti and aims at enriching Sanatan Hinduism, is taking its shape on this pious land of Ghodhara which has seen various spiritual, economic, political etc. vicissitudes and is now fortunate to illuminate the holy spirit of Vaishnava. It is pilgrimage where devotees will be able to worship Gods and Goddesses of Sanatan Vedic Hinduism along with Shri Nathji, Shri Govardhannathaji, Shri Girirajji, Shri Ganpatiji, Shri Ram Darbar (including Shri Hanuman) etc.





With the noble aim of social service and becoming a helping hand on the path of development, this institution has taken up this herculean task of building Pushtidham.


Aim:

To successfully run spiritual activities along with social, educational, economic, medical welfare activities leading to the knowledge raising in the broad scale of society by taking this task as service to the self in the form of Pushtidham.





Management:

Pushtidham will be built under the able leadership of highly respectable Param Pujya Goswami 108 Shri Dwarkeshlalji Maharajshri and with the laborious work of our source of inspiration Shri Udaybhai Desai who is the managing trusty of the trust. In order to make the spiritual heritage of his father Late Jayantilal Desai and mother Late Sumanben Desai immortal, Shri Udaybhai Desai, who is a successful, adventurous, great-lover of spiritualism, very humble human being, considers his parents as the God and the God as parents, has been blessed by the grace and inspiration of Shri Maharaj to fulfil his dream. As a result Pushtidham will consist of all the facilities along with spiritual and social activities according to the Pushtimarg and Sanatan Vedic Hindu traditions. Facts and Architectural Details of Pushtidham Pushtidham is spread in square feet and is situated on the main cross-roads of Godhara highway. One of the most attractive features of this temple is, it is surrounded by three beautiful ponds and that is why it will take form of a lotus. It will be built by focusing on the wonderful blending of water and land which is based on Hindu architecture and sculptures that will make Pushtidham the first of its kinds in entire Gujarat and will be one the pilgrimages of the country. There will various departments in Pushtidham based on different modern needs and facilities illuminating spiritual feelings. Finally it will be a common place for social, medical, educational and spiritual services and activities for the good of the people and society.
