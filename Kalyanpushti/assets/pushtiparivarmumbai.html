With the Blessings and immense feelings for the Pushtimargiya Vaishnavs of Mumbai, His Holiness P.P. 108 Shri Dwarkeshlalji Maharajshree has commenced his activities for the upliftment of Vaishnav religion in Mumbai under the name "PUSHTI PARIVAR MUMBAI"


Pushti Parivar Mumbai , came into existence in the year 2006-2007 with the Pravachan of P.P. 108 Shri Dwarkeshlalji Maharajshree which was subsequently recognized as Public Charitable Trust in the year 2009.


Presently the Trust is arranging for regular Satsang with P.P. 108 Shri Dwarkeshlalji Maharajshree on regular basis at different places in Mumbai and also 3 to 4 days Shibir at nearby places. It is also ngaged in activities of distribution of note books , education and medical to needy persons under the guidance of P.P. 108 Shri Dwarkeshlalji Maharajshree.


Pushti Parivar Mumbai has published a unique collection of Lord Shrinathji pictures in a book containing about 300 pages on different Utsavs of Lord Shreeji bawa on differenct recognized days by Mahaprabhuji.


The Trust is having its office at Nariman Point , Mumbai 400021.
