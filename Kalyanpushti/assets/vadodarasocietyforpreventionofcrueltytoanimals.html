Vadodara Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (VSPCA)


   
 

Rescue of stray and homeless animals and safe shelter. Provide hospitalization and medical care for sick / injured animals. Offer an adoption program to place animals in good responsible homes. Offer guidance and investigation concerning animal cruelty complaints. Sponsor educational and volunteer programs to promote animal welfare awareness. Organize medical camps for medical treatment and vaccination of animals.
